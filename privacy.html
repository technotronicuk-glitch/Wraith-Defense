Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Wraith Defense Corporation ("Wraith") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the data we collect. As a global defense contractor, we maintain the highest standards of data integrity.

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide our aerospace defense services, including technical telemetry, authorized personnel credentials, and procurement contact information.

2. Use of Information

Information collected is used strictly for the fulfillment of defense contracts, system maintenance, and compliance with international trade and security regulations (including ITAR and EAR where applicable).

3. Data Security

Wraith employs military-grade encryption and isolated server environments to ensure that sensitive data remains protected from unauthorized access or interception.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell data. Information is only shared with authorized government agencies or verified defense partners under strict non-disclosure agreements (NDAs).
