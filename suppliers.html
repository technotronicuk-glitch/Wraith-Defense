Integrity in the Supply Chain

Wraith Defense Corporation relies on a global network of elite suppliers to provide the raw materials and specialized components required for our S-1 Interceptor and Ghost Guard systems. We seek partners who share our commitment to precision, ethics, and sovereign security.

What We Buy

We are currently sourcing high-grade materials for our 2026 production run:

  • Aerospace-grade Carbon Fiber
  • Advanced Optical Fiber Relays
  • Solid-state LiDAR Sensors
  • High-density Lithium-Sulphur Cells

Quality Standards

All suppliers must meet or exceed our internal Wraith Quality Management (WQM) protocols, including ISO 9001 and AS9100 certification for aerospace components.

Ethics & Diversity

We prioritize suppliers who demonstrate sustainable manufacturing practices and maintain diverse, ethical workforces across the USA and UK regions.

Regulatory Compliance

As a leading defense contractor, Wraith Defense strictly adheres to International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) and the Export Administration Regulations (EAR). Prospective suppliers must be registered with their respective national defense trade controls.
