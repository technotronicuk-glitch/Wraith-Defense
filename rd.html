PROJECT PHASE: PROTOTYPE TESTING

Counter-Fiber Radar Systems

As adversary forces shift toward fiber-optic spooled UAVs to bypass traditional RF Jamming and Electronic Warfare (EW), Wraith Defense is engineering the next generation of hard-kill and soft-kill interceptors.

THE THREAT: Fiber-optic drones do not emit or receive radio signals for flight control, making them invisible to standard "Point-and-Jam" defense systems.

Active Radar Tracking

High-frequency X-band radar arrays designed to detect the physical wire-spool and the micro-signature of non-radiating drones.

Kinetic Neutralization

Our interceptor drones utilize autonomous "Net-Gun" or physical collision logic to sever the fiber-link without requiring signal interference.
